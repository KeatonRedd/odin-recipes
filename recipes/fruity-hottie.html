Fruity Hottie

Description

This is wild. I've tried with PopRocks for a bit more pizzaz but this is where the craze started. Let's get to it!

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup of Fruity Pebbles
  • 0.5 cup of chili
  • Some mayonaise
  • 1 fine frank
  • 1 raw hot dog bun

Steps

  1. Put the fine frank in the raw bun
  2. Layer the chili on top of the fine frank
  3. Squeeze some mayo on that
  4. Generously top with Fruity Pebbles
  5. Enjoy!
