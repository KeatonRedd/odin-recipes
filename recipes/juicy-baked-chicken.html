Juicy Baked Chicken

Description

What a great dish! This main dish can be prepared extremely fast and doesn't sacrifice any flavor for speed. Simple ingredients mean you hardly have to worry at all. Let's get to it.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbs brown sugar
  • 1.5 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 0.5 tsp garlic powder
  • 0.5 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder or cayenne pepper - optional
  • 4 large chicken breasts
  • 1 tbs olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425*F
  2. Using a meat mallet, beat the breasts to an average thickness
  3. Combine all the spices
  4. Line baking pan with aluminum foil
  5. Put chicken in pan. Add seasoning and olive oil.
  6. Bake chicken for 16-18 minutes, until internal temperature reaches 165*F
  7. Let chicken rest for 3-5 minutes before enjoying
